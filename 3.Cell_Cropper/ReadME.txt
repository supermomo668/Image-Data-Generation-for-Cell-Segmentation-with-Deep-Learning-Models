1. Crop by clicking on the image
	- Crop style: freestyle or by vertices

2. Keys:
	- "c" key: Toggle freestyle/vertices crop: automatically form enclosed 		contour on the points you clicked on for that crop
	- "a" key: Accept the crop: automatically form enclosed contour on the 				points you clicked on for that crop
	- "s" key: SAVE the current state of the image to their 1. crop 				coordinates (.csv) and 2. Binary mask of crop (.png)
	- "q" or Esc key: QUIT the cropping
	- "r" key: RESET the current crop after you started one.
